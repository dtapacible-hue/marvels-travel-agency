Terms & Policy

All tickets will be issued once payment has been confirmed and will be emailed thru the email address given to us by each passenger.

The following documents are needed before booking: passport copy, email address and mobile number (of each passenger).

For the Tour Packages: 50% down payment is needed once confirmed and total payment will be settled before the issuance of the hotel voucher.

Refund/Cancellation Policy

Restricted Tickets or Promo Tickets are Non-Refundable but in case of Emergency, this may be subject to the approval of the airline concerned with the support of all the necessary documents or forms to fill out as the case needed to do so.

In the case of Refund, it will be settled back to the person or company who paid for this transaction. It would take 2 to 3 months for the refund to process depending on the availability of it from the airline.

When it comes to the tour packages, it will be case-to-case basis, subject for approval of the tour operator.

Delivery of Documents

All documents for delivery are charged on the passenger’s account.
